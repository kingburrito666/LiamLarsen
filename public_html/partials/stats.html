Topics include probability, binomial and normal distributions, sampling, hypothesis testing, confidence limits, regression and correlation, and introductory analysis of variance.

Time Log

This project started after seeing a post on Reddit under r/dataisbeautiful in which someone logged every hour of their year into categories. Since New Years Day 2018 I have been logging every hour into Google Sheets, an online alternative to Excel (which has very similar functions). With categories such as "sleep, productivity, class, friends, internet, etc.," I am able to quantify hours spent doing various tasks, and map correlations between categories that seem to influence each other. I can see which months and weeks I am lacking, and days and hours that I am most productive. More information can be read in the 'About' column.
